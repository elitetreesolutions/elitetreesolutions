Directions

How to Find Us

Simple step-by-step directions from Kampala to our nursery in Kabimbiri ‚Äî whether you're driving, using a boda-boda, or asking a local.

1

Start from Kampala City Centre

Head northeast on the Jinja Road (Northern Bypass or through Mukono). The journey from Kampala takes approximately 1 hour 15 minutes by car.

2

Pass through Mukono Town

Continue through Mukono, staying on the main road heading towards Kayunga. The road is tarmac and in good condition for most of the journey.

3

Turn onto Kayunga Road

At the main Kayunga Road junction, turn and head along the Kayunga Road. Look for the signage for Kabimbiri village as you approach.

4

Find the Kabimbiri Roundabout

When you reach the Kabimbiri Roundabout, you are almost there! Our nursery entrance is exactly 500 metres from the roundabout along Kayunga Road.

5

Look for Our Sign ‚Äî You've Arrived!
